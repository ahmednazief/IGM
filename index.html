Mahmoud Nazif

Founder & Chairman

Visionary Leadership

Building sustainable ventures across three continents through strategic vision and unwavering commitment.

With over three decades of hands-on experience in mining, construction, and agricultural development, Mahmoud Nazif has built IGM into a diversified powerhouse. His journey began in the mineral-rich regions of Africa, where he recognized the untapped potential of responsible resource extraction combined with sustainable agricultural practices.

"Success in our industries requires more than capital—it demands vision, integrity, and a commitment to the communities we serve," reflects Nazif. Under his leadership, IGM has pioneered innovative approaches to integrated development, creating value chains that transform raw resources into thriving opportunities.

From establishing mining operations that set industry standards for environmental responsibility, to developing agricultural projects that feed thousands, Mahmoud Nazif's legacy is built on transforming potential into prosperity.
