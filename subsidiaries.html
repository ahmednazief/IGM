A Legacy of Excellence & Innovation Since 1991

01

Medico Misr

Construction and Road Paving Excellence

Established as our flagship construction entity, Medico Misr specializes in large-scale infrastructure projects including highway construction, urban road paving, and civil engineering. With state-of-the-art equipment and certified engineers, we've delivered over 200km of premium road infrastructure across Egypt.

Key Projects: Cairo-Alexandria Highway expansion, New Administrative Capital road networks, and multiple government infrastructure contracts.

02

Al-Ruba'iyah

Mining & Quarry Operations

Operating strategically located mines and quarries in Qena governorate, Al-Ruba'iyah extracts high-quality limestone, granite, and marble. Our sustainable mining practices combine modern extraction technology with environmental responsibility, ensuring minimal ecological impact.

Production Capacity: 50,000+ tons monthly of premium construction materials. Licensed by the Egyptian Mineral Resources Authority with full environmental compliance.

International Group for Mining and Import

Global Trading & Resource Procurement

Connecting international mineral markets with regional demand, this entity manages the importation of specialized mining equipment, raw materials, and industrial commodities. We maintain partnerships with suppliers across Asia, Europe, and Africa.

Services Include: Equipment procurement, logistics coordination, customs clearance, and supply chain optimization for mining and construction industries.

04

International Group (IE)

Comprehensive Import-Export Solutions

Our full-service trading arm handles both import and export operations, specializing in mineral exports to international markets and importing advanced machinery and technology. We facilitate millions of dollars in annual trade volume.

Export Markets: Middle East, Asia, and European Union. Certifications: ISO 9001 quality management and full MENA region trade compliance.

Arab Group for Mining and Petroleum

Energy & Natural Resources

Pioneering integrated solutions in mining and petroleum sectors. This specialized division focuses on petroleum products distribution, mining operations, and energy sector consulting. We serve industrial clients requiring both mineral resources and energy solutions.

IGM Construction

Flagship Construction & Mining Entity

The International Group for Mining and Construction serves as our primary project delivery vehicle, combining decades of experience in both mining and construction. We execute turnkey projects from feasibility studies through completion.
